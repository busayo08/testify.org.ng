About Us

TestCode, an initiative of Testify Community, is a Software Quality Assurance Engineering training program targeted towards anyone (Undergraduates, Graduates, Post-Graduates) interested in starting a career as a Software Quality Assurance Engineer. The training is a self-study program that is open to all genders and wanna-be’s Quality Assurance Engineers. Each Scholar that registers for the program will be assigned to a Mentor who will ensure that they are participating fully in the training and also making significant progress while learning. All classes will be held online for the 3 months duration across the various locations. A round-off event will be held in Lagos at the end of the program for project presentation, certification and internship placement from different partnering firm.

Our Goals

  • To equip more people in the Tech industry with skills needed to kick-start a career in Software Quality Assurance Engineering.
  • To reduce excessive unemployment status of Nigeria graduates due to the lack of required Tech skills in high demand.
  • To connect Career Beginners to successful Quality Assurance Engineers in the industry who will serve as Career guides and Mentors.

FAQs

I don't have a background in IT, can I still enrol for this programme?

Yes you can. I mean that’s the essence of learning to help you become what you want to be. However some non-technical skills like Curiosity to know, Analytical skills, Great Communication skills, Organizational skills, attitude skills and trust is an ingredient of a good software tester.

Can I join the program halfway after it has begun?:

No you can’t. This program follows a road map for each activity within the next 3 month. And completion of each week is a foot closer to becoming a software quality assurance engineer. So joining us half-way is like writing the code without understanding the business requirement

Will there be an examination after the program? If yes, How will I be graded?

There will be no examination for this program. However, applicants will be required to work on a live project and these will be graded by the professionals. Also applicants will be privileged to complete their ISTQB professional certification examination.

What does Software Testing mean?

Software Testing is a process of verifying a computer system/program to decide whether it meets the specified requirements and produces the desired results. As a result, you identify bugs in software product/project.

How skilled can I become in Software Testing after this program?

We won’t guarantee you to be 100% cooked in software testing after these programs but we are certain you will be skilled enough to resume a job as a full time staff.

Will this program have a positive impact on my career in IT?

Yes it will. A career as a quality assurance engineer is a career in IT. With this career you can easily explore other fields in IT including become a full time developer.

Will I be given a certificate after the completion of the program?

Yes you will, based on certification and the professional examination that you will complete.

Can I refer my friends to also join this program?

Yes you can, as long as they qualify for the program

What are the career prospects for me as a Software Tester?

Aside from being a Quality Assurance Engineer, you can focus on performance testing, security testing, business analyst, test automation engineer and many more.
